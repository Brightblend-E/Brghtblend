About the Company

BrightBlend Essentials is a student-run business from Tanauan City College, managed by five dedicated 4th-year students. The company is committed to creating practical, eco-friendly, and affordable cleaning solutions designed to make everyday chores easier and more enjoyable. Through innovation and sustainability, BrightBlend Essentials aims to promote a cleaner, safer, and more environment-conscious lifestyle within the community.
